Payroll & Benefits Management

Automated payroll processing with tax calculations, deductions, and benefit management.

Admin Module

Salary Structure

  • Salary Templates: Define salary components (basic, allowances, deductions).
  • Custom Rules: Set up complex tax and deduction calculation rules.
  • Pay Grades: Organize salary bands and pay grades.
  • Annual Increments: Automated annual salary increment processing.
  • Variable Pay: Manage bonuses, incentives, and commissions.

Payroll Processing

  • Attendance Integration: Auto-calculate based on attendance records.
  • Payroll Run: Batch process monthly/bi-weekly payroll.
  • Payslips: Generate and distribute digital payslips.
  • Bank Transfer: Export payroll data for bank processing (CSV/EFT).
  • Tax Deductions: Automatic income tax, professional tax, and other statutory deductions.

Leave & Attendance

  • Leave Types: Configure leave types (annual, sick, casual, LWOP, special).
  • Leave Applications: Employee portal for leave requests with approval workflow.
  • Attendance Tracking: Biometric integration or manual attendance marking.
  • Shift Management: Define shifts and manage employee scheduling.
  • Holiday Calendar: Maintain company holiday calendar.

Benefits Administration

  • Health Insurance: Manage employee health insurance enrollment.
  • Provident Fund: Track EPF contributions and balances.
  • Loan Management: Track employee loans and advances.
  • Allowances: Manage housing, food, and other allowances.
  • Deductions: Configure salary deductions for loans and other amounts.

Reporting & Analytics

HR Analytics Dashboard

  • Employee Metrics: Total headcount, new hires, exits, turnover rate.
  • Payroll Analytics: Total payroll cost, cost per employee, benefits spend.
  • Attendance Analytics: Average attendance rate, top absentees.
  • Performance Analytics: Performance rating distribution, high performers.

  • Statutory Reports: Provident fund, gratuity, income tax reports.

  • Report Scheduling: Set up recurring reports with custom frequency.
